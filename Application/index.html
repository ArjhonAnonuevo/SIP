About Us

Regulatory Vigilance

The SEC Philippines demonstrates a strong commitment to regulatory vigilance by actively monitoring and supervising the Philippine capital markets. Through effective enforcement actions and regulatory oversight, it strives to maintain market integrity, protect investors, and deter fraudulent activities.

Market Development and Innovation

The SEC Philippines plays a pivotal role in fostering market development and innovation in the Philippine capital markets. It promotes capital formation, entrepreneurship, and economic growth by facilitating fair and efficient market operations, encouraging innovation in financial products and services, and supporting initiatives that enhance market liquidity and accessibility.
